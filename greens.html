There are so many varieties of greens!

Often, people assume they do not like vegetables, or even worst never tasted many, especially greens. This is really surprising as there are so many options with a variety of tastes: sweet, bitter, crunchy, more tooth-bite, crisp, I could go on and on.

For instance, kale, cabbage, and collard greens have a tougher leaf than spinach or swiss chard. Mustard greens fall somewhere in the middle. As for taste, mustard greens are considered peppery or bitter, as is broccoli rabe (not pictured).
